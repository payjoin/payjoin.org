A couple years back, our team had an email list that gave regular updates on Payjoin's progress from Chaincase/[Payjoin Substack](https://payjoin.substack.com/). If you are receiving this as an email, it's because you were a subscriber on one of those lists.

We're the same team who has pivoted to the Payjoin solution to solve the same problem. Since then, we've worked hard to deploy [Payjoin Dev Kit](https://payjoin.org/blog/2023/06/27/pdk-an-sdk-for-payjoin-transactions), the [Payjoin Foundation](https://payjoin.org/blog/2025/08/08/announcing-payjoin-foundation) 501(c)(3), and a team of executors moving fast to bring you the next generation of this technology. If you've been out of the loop, you can try it out in [Cake Wallet](https://cakewallet.com/) or [Bull Bitcoin Mobile](https://wallet.bullbitcoin.com).

Stay tuned for email updates on Payjoin's progress, and always feel free to send us feedback, by this email, our [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/6rJD9R684h) or on [Github](https://github.com/payjoin).

Onward!
